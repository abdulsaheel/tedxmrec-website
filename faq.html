    You have questions, and we have (some) answers. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, send us a note.

    FAQ Questions

    • What is TEDxMREC?

      TEDxMREC is an independently organized TED event licensed by TED that will be held at Malla Reddy Engineering College. It aims to bring together bright minds to give talks that are idea-focused, and on a wide range of subjects, to foster learning, inspiration, and wonder – and provoke conversations that matter.

    • Is TEDxMREC event being organized by Atharva Data Science community?

      No, this event is completely independent and being organized by Md Abdul Sahil. No other community, club, or department is included in this. This is a completely student-led event.

    • How can I attend TEDxMREC event?

      Attendance details will be announced closer to the event date. Stay tuned to our official communication channels for updates on how to register and attend.

    • Will the TEDxMREC event be live-streamed?

      Yes, the TEDxMREC event will be live-streamed for those who cannot attend in person. Details on accessing the live stream will be provided closer to the event date.

    • Can I submit a proposal to speak at TEDxMREC?

      TEDxMREC follows a curated speaker selection process. If you believe you have an idea worth sharing, please stay tuned to our official channels for announcements on speaker submissions.
